1. Overview

  • Game Title: Sabble
  • Players: 2‚Äì8
  • Recommended Age: 6+
  • Play Time: ~10‚Äì15 minutes
  • Components: 55 symbol cards (each card shows 8 different symbols)

Objective: Be the first to spot and call out the one matching symbol between two cards!

2. How It Works

Any two cards in Sabble share exactly one symbol in common. Compare Card A and Card B‚Äîthere is always one and only one matching symbol. Your goal is to find that match faster than everyone else and call it out.

3. How to Play

Sabble offers multiple modes. The classic is Build the Tower, and variants include Break the Tower, Hot Potato, Card Collector, and Sabble Domino.

3-1. Build the Tower (Basic Mode)

3-1-1. Setup

  1. Shuffle the whole deck.
  2. Deal 1 card face up to each player. Place the rest face down in the center as the draw pile.
  3. Flip the top card of the pile to start the round.

3-1-2. Gameplay

  1. All players look for the one matching symbol between their card and the center card.
  2. When you spot it, call the symbol out loud immediately.
  3. The first correct caller takes the center card.
  4. Flip a new center card and continue.

3-1-3. Win Condition

  • The player who collects the most cards wins when the deck is exhausted.

3-2. Break the Tower

Setup

  1. Deal the same number of cards to all players. Each holds a personal face-down pile and flips the top card face up.

Gameplay

  1. Spot the match between your card and the center card.
  2. When correct, place your card onto the center pile.

Win Condition

  • The first to get rid of all cards wins.

3-3. Hot Potato

Setup

  1. Deal 1 card to each player. Hold it so only you can see it (face towards yourself).

Gameplay

  1. Find the match between your card and another player‚Äôs card.
  2. Call the player‚Äôs name and the symbol (e.g., ‚ÄúMinsu, Skull!‚Äù) and hand your card to them.
  3. The receiver now has more cards and must find a match quickly to pass a card on.

Win Condition

  • At the end of the round, players with no cards win; the one with the most cards loses.

3-4. Card Collector

Setup

  1. Place 1 card face up in the center.
  2. Keep the rest as a face-down draw pile.

Gameplay

  1. Flip a new card from the draw pile and find the match with the center card.
  2. The first correct caller takes the new card.
  3. Repeat, updating the center reference each time.

Win Condition

  • When the deck is gone, the player with the most collected cards wins.

3-5. Sabble Domino

Setup

  1. Shuffle all cards and deal 5 to each player.
  2. Place 1 card in the center as the starting tile.

Gameplay

  1. From your hand, play a card that shares a matching symbol with the current center/end card.
  2. The card you play becomes the new reference card.
  3. Continue clockwise, forming a chain like dominoes.

Win Condition

  • The first to play all cards wins. Fewer remaining cards are better.

4. Tips

  • Speed + Accuracy decide the game‚Äîcall what you see first.
  • Scanning the edges of a card can be faster than looking at the center.
  • With kids, slow the pace so everyone enjoys the game.

5. Safety Notes

  • Do not fold or crease the cards.
  • Keep cards away from small children‚Äôs mouths.
  • Store in a dry, cool place.
